About Us
Pet Help is an organization that aims to save stray pets by providing people with information on surrounding shelters and pet adoption centers. The creation of Pet Help is designed to better the treatment of uncared for pets around the user. The user will be give clear and organized information regarding the adoption centers around them to aid their search. We hope to not only help many families find a new companion to spend their time with, but to also offer many strays a new home for the rest of their lives
